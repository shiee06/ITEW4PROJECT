About the Philippine Art Gallery

The Philippine Art Gallery is an educational and interactive website dedicated to celebrating the vibrant history and evolution of Filipino art. Designed with modern web technologies, this platform highlights the works of renowned artists, iconic artworks, influential art movements, and cultural institutions across the Philippines.
