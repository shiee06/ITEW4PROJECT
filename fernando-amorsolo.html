Fernando Amorsolo

About Fernando Amorsolo

Known For: Master of light, rural landscapes. Fernando Amorsolo was the first National Artist of the Philippines. His iconic works include masterpieces such as "The Rice Planters" and "Harvesting Rice," which are characterized by their romanticized depictions of rural Filipino life and his mastery of natural light.

Amorsoloâ€™s vibrant works have become symbols of Philippine culture, offering a glimpse into the Filipino countryside and everyday life during the early 20th century. His distinct use of light and color made his paintings stand out, earning him widespread acclaim both in the Philippines and internationally.

Featured Paintings
  • The Rice Planters - A depiction of Filipino farm life and community.
  • Harvesting Rice - A stunning portrayal of the Filipino farmer at work.
  • El Cosechador - Celebrating rural traditions and labor in the Philippines.

Amorsolo's art is known for its bright, warm tones that symbolize hope and a positive outlook on rural life. His works bring out the beauty of everyday moments that often go unnoticed in the hustle of city life.
