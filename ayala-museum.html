Ayala Museum

Explore the fusion of culture, history, and modernity at one of the country's most innovative museums.

Overview

Located in the heart of Makati, the Ayala Museum showcases the rich culture and heritage of the Philippines, featuring dioramas, art collections, and dynamic exhibitions. The museum is known for its integration of contemporary Filipino art with digital displays, making it a must-visit for both art lovers and technology enthusiasts.
