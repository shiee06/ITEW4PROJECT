Anita Magsaysay-Ho

About Anita Magsaysay-Ho

Known For: Figurative Expressionism, modern depictions of Filipino women. Anita Magsaysay-Ho was one of the first female National Artists in the Philippines, known for her works that highlight the everyday life of women in rural settings.

Her works, often filled with vibrant colors, feature strong, expressive figures, capturing the resilience and beauty of Filipino women, especially in agricultural and domestic settings.

Featured Paintings
  • Woman with Fruits - A dynamic portrayal of Filipino women with fruits, symbolizing abundance and strength.
  • Harvesting Women - A vivid depiction of women working in the fields, celebrating their role in the Philippine agrarian society.
  • The Laundress - Showcasing the domestic duties of women in a powerful yet graceful manner.
